Praise our Lord, Dwight Fairfeild

Survivors' Goal

For you to survive, you must work with your team to repair five generators, while avoiding the killer. Usually, another survivor will be hooked, at which point it is highly recomended to save them if there isnt another goal on your mind. If you encounter an injured survivor, heal them, unless the killer is near. Repair the generators and open the gates to escape with your life.

Killer's Goal

The goal of the killer is to find and kill the survivors by hooking and therefore sacrificing them. You use your power in a unique way to benefit you while injuring, downing, and hooking survivors. The survivors will work on five generators and then open an exit and escape. You must find and chase a survivor if you see one, unless some other tactic is in mind.
